Frequently Asked Questions

What is AGORA?

AGORA is a platform that allows users to form buying groups to access exclusive discounts on luxury goods.

How do I join a buying group?

You can join a buying group by signing up on our platform and selecting a group that fits your needs.

Are there any fees to join?

No, joining a buying group on AGORA is completely free!

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact page or by emailing support@agora.com.
